LMS Workflow Example: External Customer Learning Ecosystem

Workflow: Customer Access to eLearning & ILT Packages via Salesforce + LMS Integration

  1. Customer Logs Into Support Portal
    Customer authenticates through the existing support portal. Portal permissions determine whether the user can access the Learning tab.
  2. Salesforce → LMS Auto-Provisioning
    When a customer with portal access clicks the Learning tab, the system checks Salesforce. If the user exists, an LMS account is automatically created or updated. Role, account, and entitlement data sync from Salesforce to the LMS.
  3. Automatic Access to Learning Content
    Once provisioned, the customer immediately gains access to all standard eLearning content, ILT sessions, and any learning packages associated with their account. No manual enrollment required.
  4. Purchasing Additional eLearning Packages
    When a customer selects a package to purchase, the system triggers a Salesforce workflow. An Opportunity is automatically created for the assigned Customer Success Manager. The CSM reviews the request, confirms entitlements, and completes the transaction.
  5. Credit-Based Redemption
    If the customer has training credits available, the CSM redeems them directly within Salesforce. The LMS automatically unlocks the purchased course or package and sends a confirmation email with immediate access.
  6. Reporting & Visibility
    Salesforce dashboards show opportunities, credit usage, and customer training adoption. LMS dashboards show course completions, package utilization, and training SKU performance.

Case Study: AI Agent for Automated Training Recommendations

Overview

I designed and built a Copilot agent that automated the process of recommending training packages for Sales and Customer Success teams. The agent analyzed product details, training SKUs, and delivery formats to generate accurate, scalable training recommendations.

The Challenge

Before the agent existed:

  • Sellers manually searched through training SKUs
  • Pricing was inconsistent
  • Recommendations varied by rep
  • Training capacity limits were often overlooked
  • CSMs spent time validating training details

This created friction, delays, and inconsistent customer experiences.

My Approach

I built a Copilot agent that:

  • Read from an Excel file containing all training SKUs
  • Understood duration, pricing, delivery formats, and class size limits
  • Cross-referenced training materials to ensure topic accuracy
  • Asked clarifying questions to refine recommendations
  • Generated a complete training package proposal

Key Features

Automation

Automated Selection

Topic selection, duration calculation, and pricing — all handled automatically without manual lookups.

Validation

Class Size Validation

Checks capacity limits and recommends additional sessions when needed. Intelligent follow-up questions to refine output.

Output

Salesforce CPQ Ready

Output formatted for direct entry into Salesforce CPQ — no reformatting required, faster quote creation.

Impact

  • Sellers received instant, accurate training recommendations
  • Reduced manual SKU lookup time
  • Improved pricing consistency across the sales team
  • Increased training revenue opportunities
  • CSMs spent less time validating training details
  • Faster quote creation in Salesforce CPQ

Case Study: Real-Time AI Agent for Sales Conversations

Overview

I began developing a live Copilot agent capable of supporting sellers in real time during Teams calls or chat-based interactions. The agent listened to conversations, identified training needs, and guided sellers toward the right questions and recommendations.

The Challenge

Sellers often struggled to:

  • Identify when training should be recommended
  • Ask the right discovery questions
  • Understand customer readiness
  • Connect product complexity to training needs
  • Transition from conversation to quote creation

My Approach

I designed a real-time AI agent that:

  • Listened to seller–customer conversations
  • Interpreted customer responses
  • Prompted sellers with follow-up questions
  • Identified training opportunities
  • Cross-referenced the Training SKU Recommendation Agent
  • Suggested training packages based on conversation context

Key Features

Real-Time

Conversation Analysis

Live listening and interpretation during Teams calls and chat, with intelligent question prompting for sellers.

Detection

Training Need Detection

Identifies training opportunities in real time and integrates with Agent #1 for SKU recommendations.

Output

CPQ-Ready Output

Recommendations formatted and ready for Salesforce CPQ — moving faster from conversation to quote.

Impact (Projected)

  • Increased training attach rates
  • More consistent discovery conversations
  • Reduced missed opportunities
  • Faster movement from conversation → quote
  • Stronger alignment between Sales, CS, and Training
