LMS Workflow Example: External Customer Learning Ecosystem

Workflow: Customer Access to eLearning & ILT Packages via Salesforce + LMS Integration

  1. Customer Logs Into Support Portal
    Customer authenticates through the existing support portal. Portal permissions determine Learning tab access.
  2. Salesforce → LMS Auto-Provisioning
    Clicking the Learning tab triggers a Salesforce check. If the user exists, an LMS account is automatically created or updated with role, account, and entitlement data.
  3. Automatic Access to Learning Content
    Customer immediately gains access to all standard eLearning, ILT sessions, and account-level packages. No manual enrollment required.
  4. Purchasing Additional eLearning Packages
    A package purchase triggers a Salesforce workflow and auto-creates an Opportunity for the CSM to review and complete.
  5. Credit-Based Redemption
    CSM redeems credits within Salesforce. LMS unlocks the content and sends a confirmation email with immediate access.
  6. Reporting & Visibility
    Salesforce dashboards track opportunities and credit usage. LMS dashboards track completions, package utilization, and SKU performance.
