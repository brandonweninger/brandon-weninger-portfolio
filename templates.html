LMS Workflow Example: External Customer Learning Ecosystem

Workflow: Customer Access to eLearning & ILT Packages via Salesforce + LMS Integration

  1. Customer Logs Into Support Portal
    Customer authenticates through the existing support portal. Portal permissions determine whether the user can access the Learning tab.
  2. Salesforce → LMS Auto-Provisioning
    When a customer with portal access clicks the Learning tab, the system checks Salesforce. If the user exists, an LMS account is automatically created or updated. Role, account, and entitlement data sync from Salesforce to the LMS.
  3. Automatic Access to Learning Content
    Once provisioned, the customer immediately gains access to all standard eLearning content, ILT sessions, and any learning packages associated with their account. No manual enrollment required.
  4. Purchasing Additional eLearning Packages
    When a customer selects a package to purchase, the system triggers a Salesforce workflow. An Opportunity is automatically created for the assigned Customer Success Manager.
  5. Credit-Based Redemption
    If the customer has training credits available, the CSM redeems them directly within Salesforce. The LMS automatically unlocks the purchased course or package and sends a confirmation email.
  6. Reporting & Visibility
    Salesforce dashboards show opportunities, credit usage, and customer training adoption. LMS dashboards show course completions, package utilization, and training SKU performance.

Case Study: AI Agent for Automated Training Recommendations

Overview

I designed and built a Copilot agent that automated the process of recommending training packages for Sales and Customer Success teams.

The Challenge

  • Sellers manually searched through training SKUs
  • Pricing was inconsistent across reps
  • Training capacity limits were often overlooked
  • CSMs spent significant time validating training details

My Approach

  • Built a Copilot agent reading from an Excel SKU database
  • Agent understood duration, pricing, delivery formats, and class size limits
  • Asked clarifying questions to refine recommendations
  • Generated CPQ-ready training package proposals

Impact

  • Instant, accurate training recommendations for sellers
  • Improved pricing consistency across the sales team
  • Reduced manual SKU lookup time
  • Faster quote creation in Salesforce CPQ

Case Study: Real-Time AI Agent for Sales Conversations

Overview

I began developing a live Copilot agent capable of supporting sellers in real time during Teams calls — detecting training signals, prompting discovery questions, and surfacing package recommendations mid-conversation.

The Challenge

  • Sellers struggled to identify when training should be recommended
  • Discovery question quality varied by rep
  • Transition from conversation to quote was slow and inconsistent

My Approach

  • Agent listens to seller-customer conversations in real time
  • Identifies training need signals and prompts follow-up questions
  • Cross-references the SKU Recommendation Agent for package suggestions
  • Outputs recommendations formatted for Salesforce CPQ

Projected Impact

  • Increased training attach rates
  • More consistent discovery conversations across the team
  • Faster movement from conversation to quote
  • Stronger alignment between Sales, CS, and Training
