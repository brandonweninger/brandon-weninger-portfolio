Microlearning Module: AI‑Enabled Training SKU Recommendations

This short microlearning module introduces sellers and customer success managers to the core concepts behind AI‑assisted training SKU recommendations.

Audience

Sales Representatives, Customer Success Managers

Duration

5–7 minutes

Learning Objectives

  • Explain how AI assists in generating training SKU recommendations
  • Identify the inputs required for accurate recommendations
  • Describe how iterative refinement improves quote accuracy

Module Content

1. What the Agent Does: Analyzes SKU data, training materials, and seller inputs to generate a preliminary recommendation.

2. What Sellers Provide: Product, learner count, delivery format, and any special constraints.

3. How Refinement Works: The agent asks clarifying questions, updates topics, adjusts pricing, and outputs CPQ‑ready recommendations.

Knowledge Check

Question: Which input is required for the agent to generate a recommendation?

  • A. Seller’s favorite SKU
  • B. Learner count
  • C. Customer’s annual revenue
  • D. None of the above

Correct Answer: B. Learner count

Facilitator Guide: Running an AI‑Assisted Sales Conversation

This facilitator guide supports sales enablement leaders in teaching sellers how to use AI prompts during discovery conversations.

Audience

Sales Trainers, Enablement Leaders

Duration

30 minutes

Session Goals

  • Teach sellers how to use AI prompts during discovery
  • Demonstrate how the agent listens and suggests follow‑up questions
  • Show how recommendations flow into CPQ quote creation

Materials Needed

  • Workflow diagram
  • Sample conversation script
  • Laptop with Teams/Chat simulation

Facilitator Script (Excerpt)

“As you begin discovery, speak naturally. The AI agent listens for product mentions, learner counts, and delivery preferences. When it detects a gap, it suggests a follow‑up question — for example: ‘Do you anticipate onsite or virtual delivery?’”

Practice Activity

Role‑play a seller–client conversation. The facilitator triggers AI suggestions manually using the workflow diagram.

Assessment

  • Ask at least three clarifying questions
  • Use AI suggestions appropriately
  • Review and refine the SKU recommendation
