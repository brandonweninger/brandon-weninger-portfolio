Case Studies

Real-world examples of how I build scalable learning systems, automate operations, and lead distributed teams.

Case Study 1: Scaling Technical Training Across North America & Costa Rica

Overview

As Manager of Technical Training & Learning Systems, I led a distributed team across the U.S., Canada, and Costa Rica. Our mission was to deliver consistent, high-quality technical training for a government-sector SaaS platform used in mission-critical environments.

The Challenge

Training quality varied across regions due to different workflows, inconsistent documentation, varying trainer experience, and a lack of unified SOPs.

My Approach

  • Standardized SOPs for training delivery, LMS workflows, and learner support
  • Shared templates for curriculum, assessments, and reporting
  • Weekly cross-region syncs to align on updates and quality
  • A centralized knowledge base for trainers

Impact

  • Training consistency increased across all regions
  • Onboarding time for new trainers decreased significantly
  • Cross-team collaboration improved through shared documentation
  • Leadership gained clearer visibility into training operations

Case Study 2: Building AI-Enabled Learning Workflows with Microsoft Copilot

Overview

I designed and built Microsoft Copilot agents to automate training SKU recommendations and support real-time sales conversations — reducing manual work and improving accuracy for Sales and Customer Success teams.

The Challenge

  • Sellers manually searched training SKUs with inconsistent results
  • Pricing varied by rep, creating trust issues with customers
  • Training capacity limits were frequently overlooked
  • CSMs spent significant time re-validating training details

My Approach

  • Built a Copilot agent reading from a live Excel SKU database
  • Agent asked clarifying questions and generated CPQ-ready proposals
  • Designed a second real-time agent for live Teams conversation support
  • Both agents integrated with Salesforce CPQ for seamless handoff

Impact

  • Instant, accurate training recommendations for every seller
  • Improved pricing consistency across the sales team
  • Faster quote creation in Salesforce CPQ
  • CSMs freed from manual validation tasks

Case Study 3: LMS Migration & Platform Optimization

Overview

Led a full LMS migration — from platform evaluation through go-live — while maintaining uninterrupted training delivery for internal teams and external customers.

The Challenge

  • Existing LMS was no longer meeting operational or reporting needs
  • Content library needed restructuring and quality review
  • User permissions and catalog architecture required a full redesign
  • Migration had to occur without disrupting ongoing training programs

My Approach

  • Evaluated and selected a new LMS platform based on operational requirements
  • Rebuilt catalog architecture, learning paths, and permission structures
  • Migrated and quality-checked all existing SCORM/xAPI content
  • Trained internal administrators and created platform SOPs

Impact

  • Zero disruption to active training programs during migration
  • Improved reporting capability and data visibility for leadership
  • Cleaner catalog structure increased learner self-service adoption
  • Internal admin team fully trained and self-sufficient post-launch

Case Study 4: External Customer Learning Ecosystem

Overview

Designed and implemented an external customer-facing learning ecosystem integrated with Salesforce — giving customers seamless access to training and giving the business visibility into training adoption and revenue.

The Challenge

  • Customers had no self-service access to training resources
  • Training purchases were handled manually by CSMs
  • No visibility into customer training adoption or completion rates
  • Credit-based training redemption had no automated workflow

My Approach

  • Built a Salesforce-to-LMS auto-provisioning integration via customer support portal
  • Automated access to eLearning and ILT packages at account level
  • Created a credit-redemption workflow entirely within Salesforce
  • Built dashboards for training adoption, SKU performance, and revenue tracking

Impact

  • Customers gained immediate self-service access to all training content
  • CSMs no longer needed to manually provision training access
  • Leadership gained real-time visibility into training adoption and revenue
  • Credit redemption time reduced from days to minutes

Case Study 5: Government SaaS Technical Training Program

Overview

Built and led a technical training program for a government-sector SaaS platform — delivering mission-critical enablement to government agency users where accuracy, consistency, and reliability were non-negotiable.

The Challenge

  • Government users operated in high-stakes, compliance-sensitive environments
  • Platform updates required rapid, accurate training content revisions
  • Diverse learner populations with varying technical baselines
  • Delivery had to work across ILT, vILT, and self-paced formats simultaneously

My Approach

  • Developed a modular curriculum allowing rapid updates at the topic level
  • Created role-based learning paths for different agency user types
  • Built a facilitator certification program to ensure delivery consistency
  • Established a content review cadence tied to product release cycles

Impact

  • High learner satisfaction scores across agency user groups
  • Faster content update cycle aligned to product releases
  • Certified facilitator bench reduced dependency on individual trainers
  • Training program recognized as a model for other government SaaS clients
