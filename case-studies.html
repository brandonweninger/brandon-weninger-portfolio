Case Studies

Real-world examples of how I build scalable learning systems, automate operations, and lead distributed teams.

Case Study 1: Scaling Technical Training Across North America & Costa Rica

Overview

As Manager of Technical Training & Learning Systems, I led a distributed team across the U.S., Canada, and Costa Rica. Our mission was to deliver consistent, high‑quality technical training for a government‑sector SaaS platform used in mission‑critical environments.

The Challenge

Training quality varied across regions due to different workflows, inconsistent documentation, varying trainer experience, and a lack of unified SOPs.

My Approach

I created a unified operational framework that included:

  • Standardized SOPs for training delivery, LMS workflows, and learner support
  • Shared templates for curriculum, assessments, and reporting
  • Weekly cross‑region syncs to align on updates and quality
  • A centralized knowledge base for trainers

Impact

  • Training consistency increased across all regions
  • Onboarding time for new trainers decreased
  • Cross‑team collaboration improved
  • Leadership gained clearer visibility into training operations

Case Study 2: Building AI‑Enabled Learning Workflows

Overview

I used Microsoft Copilot and Copilot Studio to modernize training operations and reduce manual workload across the training team.

The Challenge

The team spent significant time on repetitive content updates, manual reporting, learner support responses, and documentation maintenance.

My Approach

  • Automated content drafts for SOPs, job aids, and knowledge base articles
  • AI‑generated summaries of product updates for training alignment
  • Automated learner support responses for common questions
  • AI‑assisted reporting templates

Impact

  • Reduced manual documentation time
  • Faster turnaround on training updates
  • Improved accuracy and consistency
  • Freed up trainers to focus on high‑value work

Case Study 3: Optimizing LMS Administration & Learning Systems

Overview

I managed and optimized LearnUpon and Litmos LMS platforms to support thousands of learners across government agencies and internal teams.

The Challenge

The LMS needed better structure, cleaner permissions, more accurate reporting, and an improved learner experience.

My Approach

  • Created role‑based learning paths
  • Cleaned up catalogs and permissions
  • Implemented data integrity checks
  • Built reporting dashboards in Power BI
  • Standardized naming conventions and metadata

Impact

  • Improved learner navigation
  • Increased training completion rates
  • More reliable reporting for leadership
  • Reduced support tickets related to LMS confusion

Case Study 5: SOP Creation & Process Governance

Overview

I created and governed SOPs that standardized training operations, LMS workflows, and content development processes.

The Challenge

Without SOPs, teams struggled with inconsistent execution, knowledge gaps, slow onboarding, and quality variance.

My Approach

I built a scalable SOP framework that included version control, clear ownership, step‑by‑step workflows, and embedded quality checks — ensuring every team member could execute consistently regardless of experience level.

Impact

  • Consistent execution across teams and regions
  • Faster onboarding for new team members
  • Reduced knowledge-loss risk from turnover
  • Improved quality and compliance across all processes
