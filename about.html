About Me

Learning leader. Systems thinker. Operational calm.

Who I Am

I'm a learning systems and technical training leader who thrives at the intersection of technology, operations, and human learning. Over the past 15+ years, I've built and scaled training programs for SaaS platforms used by government agencies, enterprise teams, and mission‑critical operations.

My approach blends structured systems thinking, AI‑enabled workflows, and a calm, learner‑first mindset.

What I Believe

Learning should be:

Clear Supported by strong SOPs and predictable workflows
Scalable Built on systems, not heroics
Data‑Driven Measured, analyzed, and continuously improved
Human‑Centered Designed for real people in real environments
AI‑Supported Using automation to reduce friction and increase impact

Leadership Across North America & Costa Rica

I've led distributed teams across the United States, Canada, and Costa Rica. This experience taught me how to build consistent processes, shared standards, and collaborative cultures across time zones and work styles.

My Strengths

  • Turning complex systems into simple, scalable workflows
  • Building training programs that support mission‑critical operations
  • Using AI tools (Copilot, Copilot Studio) to automate and accelerate work
  • Designing LMS structures that improve learner experience and data integrity
  • Leading teams with clarity, empathy, and operational calm

What Drives Me

I love creating order out of chaos — building systems that help people learn, teams perform, and organizations scale.
