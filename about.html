About Me

Learning leader. Systems thinker. Operational calm.

Who I Am

I'm a learning systems and technical training leader who thrives at the intersection of technology, operations, and human learning. Over the past 15+ years, I've built and scaled training programs for SaaS platforms used by government agencies, enterprise teams, and mission-critical operations.

My approach blends structured systems thinking, AI-enabled workflows, and a calm, learner-first mindset.
