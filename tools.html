🛠 AI‑Enabled Job Search Tracker

This interactive job search system is a fully functional tool I designed and built using Microsoft Copilot to solve a real problem: managing dozens of applications, interviews, follow-ups, and recruiter conversations across multiple platforms.

The tracker provides a structured, user-friendly workspace that helps job seekers stay organized and confident throughout the search process.

Open the Job Tracker →

What the Job Tracker Does

Organization

Centralizes Every Application

Status tracking, dates, sources, and outcomes — all in one structured workspace.

AI

Auto-Generates Interview Prep

Uses AI workflows to generate role-specific interview questions, talking points, and research summaries.

Smart

Smart Job Recommendations

Provides targeted job recommendations based on your profile and search patterns.

Analytics

Visual Pipeline Analytics

See your application pipeline at a glance — where things stand, what needs follow-up, and what's moving.

Why I Built It

I built this tool because I was living the problem. Managing a high-volume job search across LinkedIn, recruiter calls, company portals, and networking conversations is genuinely chaotic without a system. I applied my learning operations background — SOPs, structured workflows, data visibility — to the job search process itself.

The result is a tool that treats job searching the way I treat training programs: organized, measurable, and calm.
