🛠 AI‑Enabled Job Search Tracker

This interactive job search system is a fully functional tool I designed and built using Copilot to solve a real problem: managing dozens of applications, interviews, follow‑ups, and recruiter conversations across multiple platforms.

The tracker provides a structured, user‑friendly workspace that helps job seekers stay organized and confident throughout the search process.

Open the Job Tracker →

What the Job Tracker Does

Organization

Centralizes Every Application

Status tracking, dates, sources, and outcomes — all in one structured workspace.

AI

Auto‑Generates Interview Prep

Uses AI workflows to generate role-specific interview questions, talking points, and research summaries.

Smart

Smart Job Recommendations

Provides targeted job recommendations based on your profile and search patterns.

Analytics

Visualizes Your Pipeline

Charts and dashboards to track progress across applications and interviews at a glance.

Why I Built It

I created this tool to demonstrate how AI‑enabled workflow design, systems thinking, and user‑centered design can come together to solve real operational challenges. It reflects the same approach I use when building scalable learning systems, enablement tools, and process automation solutions.

The tracker also includes fictional demo data so you can explore the full experience without entering your own information.

Training SKU Recommendation Agent

I also designed and built a Copilot agent that automates the process of recommending training packages for Sales and Customer Success teams. The agent reads from a structured Excel file containing all training SKUs, understands duration, pricing, delivery formats, and class size limits, and generates complete training package proposals.

  • Automated topic selection and duration calculation
  • Pricing and class size validation
  • Intelligent follow‑up questions to refine recommendations
  • Output formatted for Salesforce CPQ
